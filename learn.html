Learn About Your Body

Friendly education for teenagers, young women, adults, and families.

🌸 Common Symptoms

Many girls and women experience cramps, headaches, bloating, and mood swings during their periods. These are normal signs of your body adjusting each month.

🌿 Safe Hygiene During Your Period

Wash with clean water, change pads or tampons regularly, wipe front to back, and wear cotton underwear to stay fresh and healthy.

💛 Teenage Pregnancy

Sex can cause pregnancy. It’s okay to wait until you are ready. Having a baby changes your life and your goals. Seek advice and support from trusted adults.

🗓 Understanding Your Cycle

Learn how to track your days, understand ovulation, and know when you’re fertile. This helps in planning and understanding your body better.

🩸 Know About Menstruation

Menstruation is normal vaginal bleeding as part of the monthly cycle. Deal with pain using warmth, practice good hygiene, and remember: it’s NOT dirty.

🧼 Healthy Menstrual Hygiene

Change pads, wash daily, wear breathable underwear, and talk to an adult if you feel unwell. Keeping clean prevents infections.

⚠ Beware of Teenage Pregnancy

Pregnancy can lead to school dropouts, health risks, financial hardship, and limited opportunities. Protect your future by making informed choices.

👩‍❤️‍👨 Natural Family Planning

Couples can track fertile days to avoid or plan pregnancy naturally. Knowing your body and cycle is key for safe family planning.

💬 Ask for Help

If you ever feel confused or unsure, talk to a nurse, parent, or trusted adult. There’s no shame in asking for guidance and care.

🌍 Community Support

Remember, you are not alone. Your community, school, and health centers are here to help you stay safe, healthy, and informed.
